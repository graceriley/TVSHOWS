Criminal Minds

Criminal Minds is a TV show that has been on the air since 2005. It is currently on its 13th season. The show displays the lives of Behavior Analysis Unit(BAU) agents in the FBI. The members travel throughout the country to find the world's most dangerous criminals. They mostly track serial killers. They explore the minds of the criminals and observe their behavior to prove that they did this crime. The show also displays the personal lives of the agents and how this job affects them.

The cast member of Criminal Minds are Thomas Gibson (Aaron Hotchner), Shemar Moore (Derek Morgan), Matthew Grey Gubler (Dr. Spencer Reid), Joe Mantengna (David Rossi), AJ Cook (Jennifer), and Kirsten Vangsness (Penelope Garcia). Many of the cast members leave the show in different seasons, so the cast list is always changing, these are the main charcters who have been in most of the seasons.
