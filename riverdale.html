Riverdale

Riverdale is a dramatic Netflix series surrounding a group of teenagers at Riverdale High as they investigate the murder of a fellow classmate, Jason Blossom. As they dig further into Jason's case, deeper secrets about the families of Riverdale start to unravel, attracting dangerous people and presenting the high school students with limited options to uncover these secrets before they are silenced. The series premiered on January 26, 2017, and is currently on its second season of filming. The show is based off of characters that originated in the Archie Comics and is produced by CBS Television Studios and Warner Bros. Pictures.Riverdale has attracted the attention of many American teens and is currently a very popular drama and mystery on Netlfix and Fox.

The high school students who play the main characters in Riverdale are as follows:

  • Archie Andrews played by KJ Apa
  • Betty Cooper played by Lili Reinhart
  • Veronica Lodge played by Camila Mendes
  • Jughead Jones played by Cole Sprouse

The show also features subcharacters, such as:

  • Cheryl Blossom played by Madelaine Petsch
  • Josie McCoy played by Ashleigh Murray
