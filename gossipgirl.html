Gossip Girl

Gossip Girl is about seven peoples lives being followed by someone who gossips aout everything. The seven people are Serena Van Der Woodsen, Blair Waldorf,Chuck Bass, Nate Archibald, Dan Humphrey, Vanessa Abrams, and Jenny Humphry. Gossip Girl is based off of a book series and has 6 seasons. The show has won eighteen Teen Choice Awards.

The show starts with Serena Van Der Woodsen returning to New York from a bording school. She is greeted by a frenemies Blair Waldorf, Nate Archibald, and Chuck Bass. While in a fight with them, Serena meets a poor, outsider named Dan Humphrey. He has had a crush on her for many years. The show continues to follow their lives and the drama of the Upper East Side.
