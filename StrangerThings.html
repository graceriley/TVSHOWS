Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a show that is currently on Netflix. It has two seasons. It is set in Indiana during the 80's. It starts off with four boys playing Dungeons and Dragons in a basement. On a bike ride home, one of the boys goes missing. Everyone searches for him but no one realizes that he is stuck in another dimension. Along the way, the other three boys find a mysterious girl who has special powers. She helps them find will and kill the creature holding their friend.


The cast members of Stranger Things are Finn Wolfhard (Mike), Gaten Mazzarzzo (Dustin), Caleb McLauglin (Lucas), Noah Schnaap (Will), and Millie Bobby Brown (Eleven). The show mostly includes teenage actors, but some are adults. Winona Ryder (Joyce), David Harbour (Hopper), Joe Keery (Steve), Charlie Heaton (Johnathan), and Natalia Dyer (Nancy). All of these people are very talented actors. Season 1 of Stranger Things contains 8 episodes, and Season 2 contains 9 episodes.
