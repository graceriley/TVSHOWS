Friends is about a group of peopleo in their young 20s living in New York, and the next ten years of their life as they go through love, friendship, and heartbreak, among many other things. The show is an American television sitcom created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman and aired on NBC for ten seasons, from September 22, 1994 to May 6, 2004.

The main characters in Friends are as follows:

  • Rachel Green played by Jennifer Aniston
  • Monica Geller played by Courteney Cox
  • Chandler Bing played by Matthew Perry
  • Phoebe Buffay played by Lisa Kudrow
  • Joey Tribbiani played by Matt LeBlanc
  • Ross Geller played by David Schwimmer

The show also features:

  • Ugly Naked Guy played by Ugly Naked Guy
  • Ben Geller played by Cole Sprouse
